Why we built this

Every great discovery begins in someone else's footnotes. Research has always been a conversation across decades of work, but the index that conversation happens in has gotten harder to search, not easier — buried behind paywalls, scattered across journals, indexed by keywords no one actually types.

We didn't build another search box. We built a desk — a place to pull records, read what matters, and leave with a citation already in hand.

ResearchAI Pro sits directly on top of the open scholarly graph. No proprietary database, no walled garden — just a faster way to file a request and get back something worth reading.
